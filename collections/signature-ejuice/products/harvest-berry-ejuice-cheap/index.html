Full Description

When Harvest Berry was originally created I had just come out of a long spell of developing dessert and cream flavors. I wanted to return back to my roots with a natural fruit flavor, but I didn’t want to make a vape juice recipe with any of the same fruits we had used as main ingredients before.

I looked around at the flavors we had, lots of pineapple, lots of strawberry, etc. But then it came to me! We haven’t done a Blackberry Flavor yet! So I set off to create the best blackberry vape juice I could. I decided on adding sour raspberry as our second main ingredient, and then decided that the blackberries and raspberries were too tart by themselves and that the flavor could use some added sweetness to round it out. Cue the addition of blueberries!

After all was said and done, the recipe came out just like I wanted. And it even tastes amazing with the addition of menthol (cool). I decided to name it Harvest Berry, because I felt that if nature created a perfect combination of blackberry, raspberry, and blueberry, this would be what we would call it.

With one taste of this e liquid, you’ll swear you’re outside during a warm summer day. Wanna enjoy the sunshine too?
